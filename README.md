To encode:   
`encode("Any Text")`

To decode:  
`decode("Enocoded Text")`

To encode to numbers:   
`encodeNUM("Any Text")` 

To decode anything or encode anything:    
`decodeN("Anything")`

!Numbers other than encoded will remain same. It only encodes texts.
